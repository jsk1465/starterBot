The GUI will first use the messaging app s.t. they can:
    add day_times to their week calander
        /addDay day time1 time2 ...         -> confirmation message | error message
    remove day_times to their week calander
        /removeDay day time1 time2 ...      -> confirmation message | error message
    remove a particular date and time
        /removeDate date time1 time2 ...    -> confirmation message | error message
    get master calendar
        /Calendar                           -> the master calendar as na image
    get their calendar
        /myCalendar                         -> their calendar
